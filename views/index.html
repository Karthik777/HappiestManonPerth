A LITTLE ABOUT OUR COMPANY

What really motivates us is a belief that innovations change the world for better. We help innovators to finance, sell and spread word of mouth about their products globally, while consumers can get innovations faster and more tailored to their needs. We believe that we have much to offer, and we are proud of doing what we truly love.

We think that over the next few year, we will support thousands of enterpreneurs, bringing products to millions of customers. We are working hard to establish world-leading platform for innovations and smart technologies. As part of our social responsibility, we are planning to give 5% of our earnings back to innovators.

Innovations Today

We live in some of the most interesting times for innovations and creativity. The last 10 years of Internet growth created completely new business environment. We are entering a new epoch when for innovators global adoption of ideas is becoming of bigger importance than initial creation. To compete in a globalized world, innovators must focus on achieving global sales quicker and through bigger value proposition. Modern Asian and Western trading platforms, although provide a great opportunity for international trade, focus purely on commerce sides of products, leading to product commoditization and limited support of innovation development. To solve these issues, we are creating INPULZE - a new platform that provides an ideal place for innovators to focus on value proposition and for consumers to get new product quickier and better tailored to their needs.
